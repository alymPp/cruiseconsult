🚪 When you enter the cabin

Always follow this order. Interviewers ask about this directly.

  1. Knock 3 times and say "Housekeeping!" — wait 10 seconds, knock again
  2. Open the door slowly — announce yourself again if guest is inside
  3. Open windows / balcony door to ventilate the cabin
  4. Strip the bed — remove all linen, check for lost items before throwing in laundry bag
  5. Apply bathroom chemicals (toilet bowl cleaner, descaler) — let them soak while you dust
  6. Dust top to bottom — shelves, TV, nightstands, pictures, then lower surfaces
  7. Clean the bathroom — toilet → sink → shower/tub → mirrors → floor
  8. Make the bed — fresh linen, hospital corners, decorative fold
  9. Restock amenities — towels, toiletries, coffee, stationery
  10. Vacuum the floor — corners first, then main area, under furniture
  11. Final walkthrough — check everything from the guest's perspective at the door

🚿 Bathroom cleaning order

  1. Apply toilet bowl cleaner inside, let it soak
  2. Clean toilet exterior — tank, handle, base (RED cloth)
  3. Scrub inside bowl with brush, wipe seat inside and out
  4. Clean sink, faucet, counter (YELLOW cloth)
  5. Scrub shower/bathtub — apply descaler, let sit, scrub, rinse
  6. Wipe all surfaces dry to prevent water marks
  7. Clean mirrors with glass cleaner (BLUE cloth)
  8. Mop/wipe floor last
  9. Replace towels, restock toiletries, close shower curtain neatly

🎨 Color-Coded Cloth System

This is a critical interview topic. Memorise all 4 colours. The rule: NEVER mix colours — it causes cross-contamination.

🔴 RED

Toilet bowl, toilet seat, bathroom floor around toilet

🟡 YELLOW

Sink, bathroom surfaces (NOT toilet), shower, bathtub

🔵 BLUE

Mirrors, general surfaces, dusting, TV, nightstands

🟢 GREEN

Kitchen / food prep areas only

⚠️ If interviewer asks "why color coding?" — answer: "To prevent cross-contamination. Using a toilet cloth on a sink spreads bacteria and is a serious health risk, especially on a cruise ship with thousands of guests."

🧴 Cleaning Chemicals You Must Know

Know the name, purpose, and always mention: "I always read the label and follow dilution instructions."

🧪
Descaler / Limescale Remover
Removes limescale from taps, showerheads, bathtub. Apply, let soak 5–10 min, scrub, rinse.
🦠
Disinfectant
Kills bacteria & viruses. Used on toilet, sink, high-touch surfaces (handles, switches). Critical on cruise ships.
🪟
Glass Cleaner
Mirrors, windows, glass surfaces. Spray and wipe with blue cloth in circular/Z motion. No streaks.
🧹
All-Purpose Cleaner
General surfaces — desk, nightstand, TV stand, shelves. Safe for most hard surfaces.
🚽
Toilet Bowl Cleaner
Apply under rim, let soak, scrub with brush. Strong disinfectant. RED cloth for outside of toilet only.
🛡️
Air Freshener
Used last, after cleaning is complete. Light spray — not too much. Some guests have allergies.
⚠️ NEVER mix chemicals — e.g. bleach + ammonia = toxic gas. Always wear gloves. Store chemicals in original containers with labels.

Посмотри эти видео перед интервью — они объясняют именно то, о чём спросит интервьюер.

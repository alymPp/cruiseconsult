🚪 How to clean a cabin — step by step

  1. Knock 3 times. Say: "Housekeeping!" Wait. Knock again.Always knock before entering.
  2. Open the door slowly. Say your name again if a guest is inside.
  3. Open the window or balcony door. Let fresh air in.
  4. Take off all bed linen. Put it in the linen bag. Check for lost items.
  5. Put cleaning product in the toilet bowl. Let it sit. Start dusting now.
  6. Dust from top to bottom. Start high — shelves, TV, lamps. End low — floors.
  7. Clean the bathroom. See the bathroom steps below.
  8. Make the bed with clean linen.
  9. Put new towels and toiletries in the bathroom.
  10. Vacuum the floor. Start from corners. Move to the middle.
  11. Stand at the door. Look at the room. Check everything is clean and correct.

🚿 How to clean the bathroom — step by step

  1. Put toilet cleaner inside the bowl. Let it sit for a few minutes.
  2. Clean the outside of the toilet — the tank, the handle, the base. Use the RED cloth.
  3. Scrub inside the bowl with a brush. Wipe the seat — top and bottom.
  4. Clean the sink and the tap. Use the YELLOW cloth.
  5. Clean the shower or bathtub. Use descaler. Let it sit. Scrub. Rinse.
  6. Dry all surfaces. This stops water marks.
  7. Clean the mirror with glass cleaner. Use the BLUE cloth.
  8. Clean the floor last.
  9. Put new towels and toiletries. Close the shower curtain neatly.

🎨 Color-coded cloths

Use a different cloth for each area. Never mix them. This stops bacteria from spreading.

🔴 RED

Toilet only.
The bowl. The seat. The floor near the toilet.

🟡 YELLOW

Bathroom sink. Shower. Bathtub. Bathroom walls.

🔵 BLUE

Mirrors. Windows. Shelves. General surfaces in the cabin.

🟢 GREEN

Kitchen areas only. Food surfaces.

⚠️ Why is this important?
If you use the toilet cloth on the sink — bacteria moves from the toilet to the sink. This is dangerous. On a ship with thousands of guests, this can make many people sick.

🧴 Cleaning products — what they are and how to use them

Always say in the interview: "I always read the label before I use a product."

🪨
Descaler
Removes white marks from taps and showers. These white marks are called limescale. Put it on, wait 5–10 minutes, then scrub and rinse.
🦠
Disinfectant
Kills bacteria and viruses. Use on the toilet, sink, and surfaces that guests touch a lot — like door handles and light switches.
🪟
Glass cleaner
For mirrors and windows. Spray on, wipe with a blue cloth. Move in a Z shape so there are no marks.
🧹
All-purpose cleaner
For most surfaces — desk, TV stand, shelves, nightstand. Safe for hard surfaces.
🚽
Toilet bowl cleaner
Put inside the bowl under the rim. Wait. Then scrub with a toilet brush. Strong product — always wear gloves.
🌸
Air freshener
Use at the very end, after all cleaning. One or two sprays only. Some guests have allergies — do not use too much.
⚠️ Never mix chemicals together. Some chemicals are very dangerous together. For example: bleach + ammonia = poisonous gas.

Always wear gloves. Keep products in their original bottles with labels.
